Movement is for Everyone

Join the global #MoveTogether challenge. From your desk, your home, or your park. it all counts.

What is Move Together?

Move Together is a global campaign by Nike that inspires people to embrace movement in their everyday lives, no matter their age, fitness level, or background.

Who

Open to all: students, professionals, parents, seniors and everyone is welcome!

When

Launching July 2025 with a 30-day challenge and local activations.

How

Join via the Nike Training Club app or through your local event meetup. Track your progress with #MoveTogether on Instagram.
